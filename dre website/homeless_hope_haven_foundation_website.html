Homeless Hope Haven Foundation is a faith-rooted, community-driven 501(c)(3) nonprofit committed to helping individuals and families transition from homelessness to stability through compassion, resources, and empowerment.

Who We Are

Founded to serve with dignity and purpose, Homeless Hope Haven Foundation addresses homelessness through emergency relief, supportive programs, and long-term solutions.

We believe homelessness is not a choice — and with the right support, every person can rebuild their life.

Our Programs

Emergency Outreach

Hot meals, clothing, hygiene kits, and immediate crisis assistance.

Shelter & Housing Navigation

Temporary shelter coordination and assistance transitioning into permanent housing.

Stability & Life Skills

Job readiness, referrals, mentorship, recovery support, and case management.

Our Impact

  • Providing essential supplies to individuals experiencing homelessness
  • Connecting people to housing, employment, and recovery resources
  • Strengthening Iowa communities through compassion and service
